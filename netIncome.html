About this page:

This page will hold the same account held in the main tracker. The rent is based on the current daily rent while the DEC earned was based on the last 100 transaction filtered down to the last 24 hrs rewards to get the most accurate DEC earned, this will only be inaccurate if you have more than 100 DEC related transaction in the last 24 hrs.

The total showed might be different if you will manually compute each account due to the decimal factor.
